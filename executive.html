EXECUTIVE BRANCH REVIEWPAGE

  • The President is the head of the executive branch.

  • The President is the Commander in Chief of the armed forces.

  • The Vice President is the leader of the Senate.

  • The President can check Congress's power by vetoing a bill.

  • The Cabinet advices the President on many presidential matters.

  • The Executive Branch carries out the laws made by Congress.

  • The Vice president is next in the line of succession for the presidency.

  • The Speaker of the House is third in the line of succession for the presidency.

  • A president's term length lasts for 4 years.
